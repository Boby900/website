The Neon **Limited Preview** started in February, 2022, and was made available to a small number of select users and friends.

On June 15th, 2022, the Neon team announced the **Technical Preview**, making Neon available to a wider audience. During the Technical Preview, thousands of users are able to try Neon's Free Tier. For more information, see [Free Tier](/docs/introduction/free-tier).

On December 6th, 2022, Neon released its branching feature and dropped the invite gate, welcoming everyone to try Neon's Free Tier.

On March 15, 2023, Neon released paid tiers. For information about Neon's paid-tier plans, see [Billing](/docs/introduction/billing).

Support for new regions and other features will be released in the coming months.
